Peanutty Ice Cream Pie

Description

A great party pie! Top with M&Ms®, sprinkles, toasted coconut, or chocolate shavings before serving.

I'm sure you'll enjoy this very tasty pie :)

Ingredients

  • 1⅓ cups finely chopped peanuts
  • 3 tablespoons margarine, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ¼ cup peanut butter
  • ¼ cup light corn syrup
  • ¼ cup flaked coconut
  • 3 tablespoons chopped peanuts
  • 1 quart vanilla ice cream, softened

Steps

  1. Combine the peanuts, butter and sugar; press onto the bottom and up the sides of a greased 9-inch pies plate. Cover and refrigerate for 15 minutes
  2. In a large bowl, combine peanut butter and corn syrup. Add coconut peanuts. Stir in ice cream just until combined. Spoon into crust.
  3. Cover and freeze overnight or until firm.
