Mango Lemonade

Description

A mango lemonade recipe you'll fall in love with! The perfect drink when it's hot outside. Many of us don't want to fuss with making elaborate recipes so it's been my goal to come up with simple and fast recipes that are both beautiful and delicious. This is why I love this mango lemonade so much. -Ayeza Shaikh

You can't have a proper meal without something great to drink, this mango lemonade is the perfect solution!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 cups of chopped mango
  • 1 cup of lemon juice
  • 2 cups of ice

Steps

  1. To make the syrup, combine sugar and water in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil and stir until sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
  2. Combine mango and 1 cup of water in a food processor until smooth. Pour mango through a strainer into a pitcher. Stir in lemon juice and simple syrup. Mix well.
  3. Add ice to the pitcher and serve.
